CIS241 / Fall 2021 / Reflections

I appreciate the opportunity to take this class. It was interesting and challenging! Looking back on the quarter, I wish I would have taken more chances to contact the instructor and ask questions about things I didn't understand. I haven't totally gotten a handle on remote learning yet, so that is something I would like to work to improve for myself.

The class definitely felt like a good introduction to web development. I am a beginner, and after this class I feel like I am capable of extreme-beginner-level web design. It is like learning any other language, until you know enough to express what you want to express, it can feel frustrating! I am continuing the course of study next quarter, and I hope to improve my learning habits and really get some kind of grasp on this material!
